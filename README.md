Wiki Article Validator:
Ever needed to go through a bunch of Wikipedia articles and decide which ones fit some criteria? That's exactly what this tool is for.
Search a Wikipedia category, read each article in the built-in viewer, and mark them Yes or No against whatever criteria you set. Hit copy when you're done — that's it.

Built with:
Just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no install, no fuss. Data comes straight from the Wikipedia API.

Run it:
Download the files and open index.html in your browser. That's all.

How it works:
1.Enter a Wikipedia category (e.g. Mammals of India) → hit Submit
2.Click an article to load it in the viewer
3.Write your criteria in the box on the right
4.Mark it YES or NO
5.Hit Copy to grab your results.
